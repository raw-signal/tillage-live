The Harvest

The best seeds get
pressed to vinyl.

At the end of Tillage's first year, we're working towards pressing the finest collaborations onto vinyl — a permanent record of what grew here.

Every contributor gets credited. If your stem helped a seed reach Harvest, your name is in the liner notes.

By planting or watering a seed on Tillage you agree that your contribution may be included in a curated release. Full details in our terms of service. Selection is editorial — not every seed will make the cut, but every contributor will be credited on anything that does.
