The Harvest

Your music, pressed
to vinyl. Forever.

At the end of Tillage's first year, the finest collaborations will be pressed onto a physical vinyl record — a permanent document of what this community made together.

Vinyl sales help keep Tillage running — and a portion goes to a charitable cause. The record exists to honour the work — your name in the liner notes, your sound in the groove, your art on the sleeve. Contributors are offered copies at cost.

And if a collaboration is ever licensed for film, TV, or advertising — you share in that revenue too.

Every contributor is credited. Selection is editorial — not every seed will make the cut, but if yours does, it lives there permanently. Full details in our terms of service.
